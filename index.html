Bio

My name is Ken Masumoto and I'm a graduate student studying Information Management at the University of Washington, specializing in Data Science and Information Architecture. I started running in elementary school, and ran with my school teams throughout high school. After a short hiatus during my undergraduate studies, I found a revitalized passion for running in the summer of 2020, and run more now than ever. I am hoping to enter more races this coming year to break some of my old personal records. This site tracks my running progress and accomplishments.
