Information Management is the study of finding the best ways to get the correct information to the correct people within companies. I'm specializing in Data Science and Information Architecture. Data Science uses programming and algorithms to find things out about large sets of data. Information Architecture has to do with the way things on structures on things like websites to create as little confusion as possible.
